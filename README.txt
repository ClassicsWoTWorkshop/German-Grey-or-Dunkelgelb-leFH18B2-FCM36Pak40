To install:
- Drag the "gui", "scripts," and "vehicles" folders from the folder with the vehicle & color option of your choice to your World of Tanks install directory (by default, C:\Games\World_of_Tanks\res_mods\<version>) (example: C:\Games\World_Of_Tanks\res_mods\0.9.22.0).

Enjoy!
